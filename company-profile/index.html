SSLT INTERNATIONAL GROUP

SSLT INTERNATIONAL GROUP is a world-leading manufacturing and service supplier of EAS, RFID intelligent recognition, and open-shelf experience marketing systems. SSLT strives for perfection with technological innovation, fine workmanship, preeminent quality, sincere service and good faith in building a top EAS industry brand. SSLT insists on practical and professional services to provide customers with the smartest products and best solutions.

SSLT delivers top anti-theft solutions to retail, open-shelf and limited-access sites worldwide – including universities, colleges, chain clothing stores, and any enterprise limiting access to certain confidential assets. Based in Mainland China, SSLT exports to over 60 nations across Europe, The Americas, The Middle East and Asia.

Domestically, SSLT has won numerous honors such “China Famous Brand”, “China Top Brand”, “China Green Environmental Protection Product”, “China Top 10 EAS Brand”, “3A Brand for Quality, Service and Reputation”, “National Quality Trustworthy Product by China National Accreditation Service for Conformity Assessment”, etc.

SSLT CORPORATE CULTURE

SSLT INTERNATIONAL GROUP

SSLT firmly grasps the IOT industry’s future development trend – placing RFID as its core to provide intelligent inventory, security, delivery, tracking, and service. SSLT truly understands modern customer demand, so as to guide end-users in intelligent product use and lead the industry’s technological innovation. Our unswerving pursuit is to make SSLT a first-rate international brand.

SSLT is an outstanding global brand specialized in open-shelf security for libraries, book stores, supermarkets, clothing stores, cosmetics stores, drugstores, etc. SSLT produces the market-leading LPMS security system, and is a global EAS solution provider.

SHOWBAO is a global brand for antitheft devices, digital experience and 3G service marketing, and retail household appliance display solutions. ShowBao’s antitheft, wire break alarm, real cellphone charging, and on-site cell phone experience products are used in domestic and overseas retail displays by China Telecom, China Mobile, China Unicom, Lenovo, Samsung, Apple, Suning, Gome, Dazhong, Zoomflight, and many other companies.

IOTRFID is a global brand for RFID management equipment; automated library circulation management solutions (RFID library self-service book-borrow & return systems, library collection workstations, label transition systems, intelligent security detection systems, etc.); confidential asset protection; clothing antitheft; hospital, device, and inventory tracking; access control; and tracking of babies, pets, prison inmates, etc.

APPLICATIONS OF SSLT PRODUCTS INCLUDE

LIBRARIES

SSLT has become the leading brand for library tracking. SSLT is a designated brand for equipment biddings in nationwide universities, and SSLT products have been adopted by the Chinese Academy of Governance, Renmin University, China Central Academy of Arts, The Central Academy of Drama, National Prosecutors College, and other national first-rate universities.

CONFIDENTIAL ASSET PROTECTION

SSLT has long served the Chinese asset protection market. From EM, RF, AM systems, to the current RFID systems, SSLT has successfully provided The Second Artillery Force of CPLA, CPLA General Political Department, General Headquarters of the Air Force of CPLA, and various military commands with protection for USB flash disks, laptops, documents, and other assets.

CLOTHING STORES

SSLT EAS products are in use in clothing stores in China and in more than 60 countries across the world. SSLT is the sole supplier for Bestseller Fashion Group (with popular brands such as JJ, ONLY, VM, and SLT), and has already consolidated its status as the leader in garment anti-theft industry.

SUPERMARKETS

EAS products are essential to supermarket security, and the SSLT brand maintains a large and solid customer base in supermarket anti-theft.

OPEN-SHELF DIGITAL PRODUCT MANAGEMENT

ShowBao products are sold in over 60 foreign countries, and for more than three years successfully provided to China Telecom, China Mobile, China Unicom, Apple, Samsung, Lenovo and other digital product stores for security protection.

PERSONNEL MANAGEMENT

SSLT RFID products improve efficiency and safety in hotels, public VIP spaces, or places with babies or senior citizens.
